Hi, I'm Maddie

digital curator / metadata specialist / archives & records manager / maker

Hi, I’m the Maddie behind maddiefactured, and I am passionate about information delivery and accessibility. I am interested in archiving and preserving data and digital content, in order for it to be findable and usable for interested audiences. My focus is on managing and manipulating metadata with a particular interest in the delivery of data and metadata. I’m interested in exploring how metadata and knowledge organization can make collections and records more accessible.


Beyond the metadata realm, I am also interested in techniques of making and crafts, with a particular interest in fiber arts such as knitting and crocheting. I like to explore ways in which cultural institutions can leverage making and hacking movements to increase audiences and participation with their collections.
