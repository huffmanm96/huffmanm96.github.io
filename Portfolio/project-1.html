My Experience

I have five years of experience working in museums, archives, and libraries. My greatest interest and experiences lie in making collections and records for research organizations available to be found and reused via delivery of metadata, data sets, and digital content. For more information on my previous experiences, see below for example projects and skills.

What I've Done

EAD Creator Python Tool

Built a dynamic Python program to transform data spreadsheets into EAD valid XML documents, for import of collections data into Archivists’ Toolkit (AT) database.

Removable Media Transfer

Used digital forensics techniques to transfer content off of a variety of removable media, for long-term preservation of and access to collections. Described transferred content via metadata files and FileMaker Pro collections database.

Collections Information Specialist

Strategized and implemented best practices for collections information system and digital asset management system to support museum-wide projects and procedures. Executed data manipulation, migration, and analysis techniques to provide support during cataloging and data conversion projects. Specialized in reporting information out of systems to provide all users with access to collections information in practical formats and support the extraction of data for delivery to the online collections catalog.

“Deconstructing Components” Presentation & Webinar

Presented at the Collective Imagination conference on the use of components in TMS, focusing on the cataloguing and delivery of information via Crystal Reports for components. Included follow-up global webinar presentation to reach more users.
